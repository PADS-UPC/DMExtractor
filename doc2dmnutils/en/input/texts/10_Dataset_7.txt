If the order is above 5 units, the customer number starts with 0 and the day is Tuesday, then a discount of 5% is given.

A discount of 10% is given whenever the order exceeds 10 units, it is weekend and the customer number begins with 5.

When the financial situation is good, the customer has an income and the loan is less than 1000 euro, the applicant risk rating is low.

The risk rating is medium if the financial situation is moderate, the customer has a business and the loan exceeds 1100 euro.

The service request is a product change if the score is 10, the technical analysis was easy and the amount of hours needed is 9.

In case the score is 5, the technical analysis was difficult and the estimated work time is less than 5 hours, then the service request is a bug.
